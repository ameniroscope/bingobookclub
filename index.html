Welcome to our book club — where fantasy worlds are serious business, and everything else is up for debate.

For each book, you fill out your own bingo card before you start reading. Try to predict the tropes, plot twists, and moments that will make you gasp, laugh or roll your eyes… As you read, mark your squares, question your choices and realize you probably should have trusted your gut and not common sense.

Every so often, we will meet. Expect deep dives into world-building, chaotic predictions and very strong opinions about whether something truly deserves a bingo square.

Read, react, mark your card and come ready to discuss everything.
